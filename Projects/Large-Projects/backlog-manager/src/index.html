Xenoblade Chronicles 3 is an incredible entry in the series. Incredible gameplay, top-notch OST, a compelling but slightly rushed story, with a lot of thematical depth. The characters are incredible, with some impressive development and easy to get attached to. 3's biggest flaw is the fact that, for as good as everything is, it fails to be as memorable as 2 in most aspects, and it's awful antagonists
